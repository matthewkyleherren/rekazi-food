About us

Rethinking legal work

At Leya, we believe in lowering the barriers to technology within the legal sector. Artificial Intelligence won't replace lawyers anytime soon, but lawyers using it might. We prioritize building something people love, and can get started with today.

Let’s cut through the noise. You didn’t get into law to drown in paperwork. You’re here to make a dent in the universe, to solve complex problems that demand more than just your ability to read fine print. What if you could focus on what you're truly great at?

We founded Leya with the vision of giving you as a lawyer the tools you deserve. Leya transforms your practice, doing the grunt work so you can concentrate on what computers can’t: human intuition, ethical judgment, creative problem-solving.

Trust is the cornerstone of your profession. Leya enhances that trust, designed with unerring accuracy and absolute confidentiality in mind. In turn, we are entrusted by legal professionals who are determined to stay in front of their competition and bold enough to take a leap into the future.

We didn't build Leya for how law is practiced today; we built it for how it should be practiced tomorrow.

– The team at Leya
