At Leya, we are revolutionizing the legal landscape with cutting-edge AI technology designed to streamline and enhance legal operations. Founded in 2023, we have quickly gained recognition for our innovative approach, culminating in a successful $10.5 million seed round led by Benchmark, with participation from Y Combinator, Hummingbird, and SV Angel.

‍

Our AI-powered legal assistant integrates seamlessly with law firms' document management systems and leverages data from over 15 jurisdictions, providing precise citations and references from legal databases. This multi-feature tool enhances productivity by automating tedious tasks, allowing legal professionals to focus on strategic and impactful work.

‍

With a growing team and over 70 customers across Europe, Leya is dedicated to transforming the legal profession by unlocking valuable knowledge, improving outcomes, and increasing efficiency. Our commitment to data privacy and confidentiality ensures that our AI solutions meet the highest standards, making us a trusted partner in the legal tech space.

Join us as we continue to innovate and shape the future of legal work, bringing state-of-the-art AI solutions to law firms and in-house legal teams worldwide.
